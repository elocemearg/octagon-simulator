About

This is a mockup of the on-screen countdown clock used by various BBC shows in the 1990s. It was prompted by this video about the BBC Micro with Tom Scott and the Centre for Computing History.

The font for the numbers was reverse-engineered from screenshots of old shows, probably not quite accurately.
